About Me

I am a dedicated Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Student at Southeast University (Class of 2023) and an active, long-standing member of the World Scouting movement since 2011. Currently, I am serving as the Senior Rover Mate at Crystal Open Scouts, where I lead with a focus on personal, organizational, and community development.

I am immensely proud to be part of the very first batch in Bangladesh to receive the prestigious Messenger of Peace (MoP) Ring Badge. My journey has spanned across national levels, including serving as the Former Deputy Coordinator of the Public Relations & Marketing National Taskforce at Bangladesh Scouts. With a strong foundation in IT and Graphics Design, having served as IT Secretary at Southeast Cultural Society, I combine technical expertise with seasoned leadership to drive impactful results.
